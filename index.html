RCPanel

Welcome to RCPanel, our smart solar tracker designed to maximize energy saving.

RCPanel is a smart solar tracking and monitoring system developed as an Arduino-based solar panel prototype integrated with an Android mobile application. The system is designed to optimize solar energy harvesting through automated panel orientation based on real-time sunlight detection. Using Light Dependent Resistors (LDRs) as sensors, the Arduino microcontroller processes light intensity data and dynamically adjusts the panel position via servo motors to maintain optimal alignment with the sun.

RCPanel supports both automatic tracking mode and manual control mode. In automatic mode, the system autonomously adjusts panel orientation based on sensor inputs. In manual mode, users can remotely control the panel positioning through the mobile application. Additionally, the system provides real-time notifications and performance updates to enhance monitoring reliability and user awareness.

By integrating embedded systems, sensor technologies, wireless communication, and mobile application development, RCPanel demonstrates a practical implementation of renewable energy optimization and smart automation. The project highlights the application of microcontroller-based control systems in improving solar energy efficiency while promoting accessible and sustainable energy management solutions.

At the bottom of the page, users are provided with access to download the Android application for direct interaction with the system, enabling remote monitoring, control, and performance evaluation in real time.
