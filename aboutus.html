About Us

RCPanel is a capstone project developed by Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) students from Tarlac State University, specializing in Web and Mobile Applications. The project was designed to integrate software and hardware technologies in creating an innovative solar tracking system that improves energy efficiency through automation.

Our team focuses on combining Arduino-based hardware components with an Android mobile application to provide real-time monitoring, control, and data tracking. Through this system, users can automatically or manually adjust the position of solar panels to maximize sunlight exposure and optimize energy generation.

RCPanel reflects our commitment to applying technical knowledge in programming, mobile development, and embedded systems to address real-world challenges in renewable energy. The project emphasizes accuracy, efficiency, user accessibility, and practical implementation.

Mission

To design and develop a smart solar tracking system that utilizes Arduino and Android technologies to improve solar energy efficiency, provide real-time monitoring, and promote sustainable energy solutions.

Vision

To contribute to innovation in renewable energy by creating reliable, user-friendly, and technology-driven solutions that support sustainability and smart automation.
